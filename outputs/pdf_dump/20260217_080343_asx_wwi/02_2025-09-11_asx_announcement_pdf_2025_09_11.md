WEST WITS MINING LIMITED (ASX:WWI) – REQUEST FOR TRADING HALT 
 
West Wits Mining Limited (ASX:WWI) (the Company) hereby requests a trading halt in relation to the 
Company’s securities quoted on the ASX in accordance with Listing Rule 17.1. 
 
The trading halt is requested pending an announcement by the Company to the market regarding a 
proposed capital raising (the Purpose). 
 
In accordance with Listing Rule 17.1, the Company provides the following information in relation to 
the request: 
1) The trading halt is necessary to assist the Company in managing its continuous disclosure 
obligations as the Company expects to make an announcement to the market in relation to the 
Purpose stated above. 
 
2) The Company requests that the trading halt remain in place until the earlier of the Company 
releasing an announcement regarding the Purpose or the commencement of normal trading on 
Monday, 15 September 2025. The Company expects to make the announcement to the market 
before the commencement of normal trading on Monday, 15 September 2025. 
 
3) The Company is not aware of any reason why the request for the trading halt should not be 
granted or of any other information necessary to inform the market about the trading halt. 
 
Please contact me if you require any further information.
